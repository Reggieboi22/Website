GREETINGS

We pride our business venture as a never before seen rest spot to enjoy a multitude of hot and cold caffeinated drinks with the entertainment of competitive Slap Fighting, a new sport taking the internet by storm by its uniqueness as intertainment also its simplicity and inclusivity of participation.

View our delicious store made beverages and the schedule of our weekly slap contest to ether participate or watch with tingling excitement the contestants giving their all in every blow.
