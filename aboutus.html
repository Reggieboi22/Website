WHO ARE WE?

Slapshot Coffee Shop provides the highest quality sips of caffeine made lovingly in house with ingredients provided by Allied Coffee Corp. paired with charming spectacle you definitely need to see. Located across the street from Movati Athletic, one of Ottawa's top 3 gyms where the most of our contenders arrive from to test themselves for your entertainment. Hence why this is a one of a kind experience only we can offer you.
